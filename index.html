Meet Pat!

My name is Pat Fish, and I'm the proud owner and founder of Pat's Salmon Cookies.

Made with only the highest quality local salmon, my cookies are a little taste of the Northwest you and your family will love.

  • SeaTac Airport (open 10a-6p)
